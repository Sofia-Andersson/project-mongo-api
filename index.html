IKEA PRODUCTS

Choose a path to find the IKEA products information you are looking for!

/products : To view all products

/products/name/:name : Search for a product by name. Example route: /products/name/stig

/products/lowestprice : See all products listed by lowest price first

/products/designer/:designer : Search for products by designer name. Example route: /products/designer/Francis Cayouette
